Case Study

Modern Bungalow: Customer Communication That Runs Itself

15+ hours monthly saved with automated Shopify email workflows across 9 different customer scenarios.

The Challenge

Modern Bungalow was manually sending 30-40 customer emails daily. Order confirmations, shipping updates, back-in-stock notifications - all handled by hand.

Things were falling through the cracks. Customers weren't getting timely updates. And someone was spending hours every day on repetitive emails.

The Solution

9 Automated Email Scenarios

Order confirmations, shipping updates, delivery notifications, back-in-stock alerts, review requests, and more - all triggered automatically by Shopify events.

Event-Based Triggers

Emails send at exactly the right moment. Not batched overnight - triggered instantly when events occur.

Consistent Branding

Professional, on-brand emails that match their customer experience. Every time, without variation.

The Results

  • 15+ hours monthly saved on customer communication
  • Zero missed emails - every customer gets the right message
  • ROI in under 8 weeks
  • Better customer experience with faster, more consistent updates
