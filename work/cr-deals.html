CR Deals Cincinnati: $52K Annual Savings

AI system that analyzes product photos, generates descriptions, and posts to all sales channels. 50-100 products daily, no human needed.

The Challenge

CR Deals was drowning in product listing work. Every item that came through their auction house needed photos analyzed, descriptions written, condition assessed, and listings posted to multiple channels.

The manual process was eating hundreds of hours monthly. They needed to scale but couldn't hire fast enough.

The Solution

AI Photo Analysis

AI examines product photos to identify items, assess condition, and extract relevant details for descriptions.

Automated Description Generation

Based on the photo analysis, the system generates compelling, accurate product descriptions optimized for each sales channel.

Multi-Channel Distribution

Listings automatically formatted and posted to all relevant marketplaces and channels simultaneously.

The Results

  • $52K annual savings in labor costs
  • 1,040+ hours back per year
  • 50-100 products listed daily without human intervention
  • Paid for itself in the first month
