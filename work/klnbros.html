Case Study

KLN Bros: Emergency Server Recovery

When their server crashed and the database corrupted, they were facing potential total data loss. We recovered the system and implemented proper disaster recovery.

The Challenge

Friday afternoon. The call came in: "Our server won't start. Everything is on there - customer records, invoices, inventory. We can't operate."

The server had crashed, and worse, the database had corrupted during the improper shutdown. Their backup system? It had silently failed months ago. No one had noticed.

The Solution

Emergency Response

Dropped everything and started working immediately. In crisis situations, every hour matters. Remote access was established within 30 minutes.

Database Recovery

Used specialized tools to repair the corrupted database. Recovered 99.7% of the data - only a handful of records from the day of the crash were unrecoverable.

System Restoration

Rebuilt the server environment, restored the database, verified data integrity, and got them back online. Business operations resumed.

Proper Backup Implementation

Implemented automated backups with verification, off-site replication, and monitoring alerts. Never again.

The Results

  • 99.7% data recovered from corrupted database
  • Business back online within 48 hours of the crash
  • Automated backups now running with verification
  • Monitoring alerts ensure backup failures are caught immediately

Client Feedback

"When Seth called me back within 10 minutes on a Friday evening and said he could help, I knew we'd be okay. By Monday morning we were back in business. He saved our company."

Lessons Learned

This situation was preventable. Here's what we implemented to ensure it never happens again:

  • Automated daily backups with retention policies
  • Backup verification - actually test that backups work
  • Off-site replication - local backups aren't enough
  • Monitoring and alerts - know immediately when something fails
  • Documented recovery procedures - have a plan before you need it
