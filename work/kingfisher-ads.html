Case Study

Kingfisher Ads: Reporting That Runs Itself

Automated monthly ad spend reports pull from Google Ads and Meta, calculate performance metrics, and land in clients' inboxes on the 1st of every month.

The Challenge

Every month, the same drill. Log into Google Ads. Export data. Log into Meta. Export more data. Open spreadsheets. Calculate metrics. Format reports. Send to clients. Repeat for every client.

Hours of work that produced the same report structure every single time. Just with different numbers.

The Solution

Automated Data Collection

System connects directly to Google Ads and Meta APIs. Pulls spend, impressions, clicks, conversions, and ROAS automatically.

Intelligent Report Generation

Data flows into branded report templates. Metrics calculated, charts generated, insights highlighted - all without manual intervention.

Scheduled Delivery

Reports automatically sent to clients on the 1st of each month. Professional, consistent, and on time - every time.

The Results

  • Hours saved monthly on report generation
  • Zero missed deadlines - reports always arrive on time
  • Consistent formatting across all client reports
  • More time for strategy instead of data pulling

Client Feedback

"The reports used to take me half a day. Now they just happen. I actually forgot it was the 1st until I saw the reports had already gone out."
