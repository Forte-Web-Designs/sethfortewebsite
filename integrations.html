YOUR SYSTEMS DON'T TALK TO EACH OTHER.

Your CRM says one thing. Your spreadsheet says another. Your team spends half their day copying data between systems that should be connected. I build integrations that make it flow automatically.

WHAT THIS IS ACTUALLY COSTING YOU

Every time someone copies data from one system to another, you're paying twice: once for their time, and again when the inevitable error creates rework.

Different sources of truth. Sales says the deal closed. Finance says it didn't. Customer success has a different number entirely. Nobody trusts the data because nobody knows which system is right.

Manual data entry everywhere. New lead comes in through the form, someone copies it to the CRM, someone else adds it to the project tracker, another person updates the spreadsheet. The same information, entered four times.

Decisions based on stale data. By the time your weekly report is compiled, half the numbers have changed. You're making decisions based on last week's reality.

HOW I APPROACH THIS

Start with what needs to connect and why. Not every system needs to talk to every other system. The goal is eliminating the manual work and data conflicts that are actually costing you money.

I map the data flow. Where does information originate? Where does it need to go? What triggers the need for it to move? Once we understand that, the technical solution becomes obvious.

Sometimes the right answer is a simple Zapier connection. Sometimes it's a direct API integration. Sometimes it's a custom middleware that handles complex transformations. I pick the right tool for the job, not the one I'm most comfortable with.

5 years at Wells Fargo building systems where downtime costs six figures an hour. I know how to build integrations that don't break.

WHAT THE SOLUTION LOOKS LIKE

CRM & Sales Integration

Leads flow automatically from your forms to your CRM. Sales activities sync to your reporting. Customer data stays consistent everywhere it lives.

E-commerce & Operations

Orders from Shopify, Amazon, or your website sync to inventory, accounting, and fulfillment. No more manual updates, no more overselling.

Marketing Stack

Form submissions, email engagement, ad performance - all flowing into one place. Your marketing team stops switching between 12 tabs.

Project Management & Team Tools

New deals create projects. Completed tasks update clients. Slack notifications trigger at the right moments. Your tools work together instead of in parallel.

Custom API Connections

When there's no native integration and Zapier can't do it, I build direct API connections. Your workflow isn't limited by what's available off the shelf.
