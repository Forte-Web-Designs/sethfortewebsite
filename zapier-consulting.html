ZAPIER IS THE RIGHT TOOL FOR A LOT OF JOBS.

6,000+ app connections. Simple triggers and actions. Sometimes that's exactly what you need. Sometimes you've hit its limits and need help figuring out what's next.

WHEN ZAPIER IS THE RIGHT CHOICE

Zapier isn't always the answer, but sometimes it's exactly right:

  • Huge app library: 6,000+ apps. If you need to connect two SaaS tools, Zapier probably has both.
  • Simple workflows: Trigger → Action. When that's all you need, why overcomplicate it?
  • Tables & Interfaces: Build simple apps without code. Good for internal tools.
  • Team-friendly: Non-technical team members can understand and maintain simple Zaps.
  • It just works: For basic use cases, setup is minutes, not hours.

If your needs fit these criteria, Zapier is probably the right choice. I can help you build it right the first time.

WHEN ZAPIER HITS ITS LIMITS

Zapier is great until it isn't. Common pain points:

"I need complex branching logic." Paths help, but complex conditional logic gets awkward fast. Make.com or n8n handle this better.

"My costs are scaling out of control." Task-based pricing means high-volume workflows get expensive. At some point, alternatives become cheaper.

"I need better error handling." Zapier's error handling is basic. When reliability matters, you need more control.

"The connector I need doesn't work right." Zapier connectors are built for broad compatibility, not depth. Sometimes you need a custom integration.

"I need to self-host." Zapier is cloud-only. If data sovereignty or security policies require self-hosting, it's not an option.

If you're hitting these limits, there are alternatives. Compare your options here.

WHAT I BUILD IN ZAPIER

Multi-Step Zaps

Workflows that go beyond simple trigger-action. Paths, filters, formatters, and multiple actions chained together.

Tables & Interfaces

Simple internal apps built on Zapier's no-code tools. Databases, forms, and dashboards without custom development.

Webhook Integrations

When the native Zap doesn't exist, webhooks often can. I build custom webhook integrations for apps that aren't in the library.

Optimization

Reducing task usage, consolidating Zaps, and structuring things so they're maintainable. Keeping costs predictable.

Migration Planning

When you've outgrown Zapier, I help plan the migration to Make.com, n8n, or custom solutions.
