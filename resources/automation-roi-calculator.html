Beyond the Spreadsheet

Some things are worth doing even when the ROI isn't instant.

Strategic value - A client portal might not save hours, but it makes you look more professional than competitors. That's positioning, not just productivity.

Foundation for growth - You can't scale chaos. Sometimes you fix something small so you can grow into something big.

Quality of life - There's value in not thinking about something anymore. In knowing it just happens. That's hard to put a dollar sign on, but it's real.

Compound effect - Some automations unlock others. Fix the CRM, and suddenly reporting is possible. Fix reporting, and suddenly insights emerge. One system enables the next.

The calculator is a starting point, not the whole picture.
