What is Business Automation?

Software that does repetitive tasks automatically.

Form filled out → contact created in CRM.
Order placed → confirmation email sent.
Deal closed → project tasks created.

That's it. No robots. Just systems that handle the boring stuff.

When you need it

  • Someone does the same task repeatedly.
  • Data gets copied between systems.
  • Things fall through the cracks.

When you don't

  • Task requires judgment every time.
  • Happens rarely.
  • The process itself is broken.

The math

1 hr/day at $35/hr = $9,100/year.
Automation costs $3,000-5,000.
Payback: 4-8 weeks.
