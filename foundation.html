What I build

CRM and Sales Pipeline

▼
Having a CRM login is not the same as having a CRM. If your pipeline is a mental model and leads get added manually when you remember, you don't have a system. I build the real thing: stages, tags, custom fields, and automation that moves deals forward without you touching it.

Payment Processing

▼
A customer pays through Stripe. Nothing happens. No CRM update, no team notification, no next step triggered. I connect your payment processor to the rest of your stack so every transaction fires the right action automatically.

Email Infrastructure

▼
Your emails are landing in spam because nobody configured SPF, DKIM, and DMARC on your domain. I fix that before you send a single campaign so your messages actually reach the inbox.

Booking and Scheduling

▼
Your booking tool isn't connected to your CRM. Someone books, you get an email you might see in time, and there's no intake form, no reminder, no pipeline record. I wire scheduling into everything else so bookings are tracked, confirmed, and followed up automatically.

Tracking and Attribution

▼
You're spending money on ads and you can't tell which ones work. I install analytics, pixels, conversion events, and UTM tracking so every lead source is identifiable in your CRM. Decisions based on data, not gut feel.

Workflow Automation

▼
If your team does the same task every week on autopilot, the system should too. I automate the repetitive work so they don't have to.

Client Onboarding

▼
You close a new client and then spend two days chasing credentials, sending welcome emails manually, and forgetting to set up the project. I automate the full sequence so contract signed triggers everything: welcome email, intake form, kickoff scheduling, project setup, team notification.

Integrations and Data Sync

▼
You're paying for six tools that don't know the other five exist. I connect them so data flows where it needs to go. When something happens in one system, the rest of your stack knows about it.
