Your system, built. Your first leads, flowing.

Most businesses come to me with contacts in spreadsheets, a CRM nobody set up, and no system to capture leads. Foundation fixes all of it. You get the infrastructure AND leads start coming in from day one.

  • We have thousands of contacts but no pipeline or segmentation
  • We're paying for a CRM but nobody configured it properly
  • Our tracking is broken and we can't tell which marketing actually works
  • We need to accept payments, book calls, and manage leads in one place

What you walk away with

Lead Capture

Your first lead source connected and producing. Whether that's a Meta ad, a website form, or a landing page opt-in, leads flow directly into your CRM, tagged by source, with an instant auto-response and team notification. No empty pipelines.

CRM and Pipeline

Sales pipeline with stages that match how you actually sell. Contact tags, custom fields, and segmentation so you can find anyone and know where they are.

Payment Processing

Stripe or your processor connected. Products configured. Receipts and confirmations automated. You can take money on day one.

Email Infrastructure

Domain authentication (SPF, DKIM, DMARC) so your emails land in inboxes, not spam. Sending domain verified and deliverability confirmed.

Booking and Intake

Calendar connected for scheduling. Intake forms or applications built and routed. No more back-and-forth emails to book a call.

Tracking

Google Analytics or Meta Pixel installed. Basic conversion events configured. You'll know where traffic is coming from and what it's doing.

Integrations

1-2 core integrations connecting your most important tools. Calendar, forms, payment processor, whatever needs to talk to the CRM.

Documentation and Training

System overview document, credentials, a Loom walkthrough of everything, and post-handoff support so you're not lost after delivery.
