Let's Talk

YOUR TEAM IS SPENDING HOURS ON WORK A COMPUTER SHOULD HANDLE.

Data entry. Follow-ups. Reporting. The same tasks, over and over, while the work that actually matters sits waiting. I build automation that eliminates manual work and pays for itself in 4-8 weeks.

WHAT THIS IS ACTUALLY COSTING YOU

Every hour your team spends on repetitive tasks is an hour they're not spending on work that grows your business. But the real cost is bigger than the hours.

Things fall through the cracks. When follow-ups depend on someone remembering, some get forgotten. When data entry is manual, errors creep in. When reporting is a slog, it gets delayed or skipped.

Scaling means hiring. If every new customer means more manual work, growth gets expensive. You're paying people to do work that a system could handle once, forever.

Your best people are doing grunt work. The person who's great with customers is spending half their day copying data between systems. That's not what you hired them for.

HOW I APPROACH THIS

Before we talk about what I'll build, I need to understand what the problem is costing you. Hours per week. Labor costs. Missed opportunities. Errors that create rework. Once we know that number, everything else follows - including exactly when you'll see your investment back.

If the math doesn't work, I'll tell you - even if it costs me the project. Not every process should be automated. Some things aren't worth the build. I'd rather lose the project than build something that doesn't pay for itself.

The technical build is the easy part. I work with n8n, Make.com, Zapier, and direct API integrations - whatever fits the problem. What you're paying for is someone who understands your business, builds for where you're going, and turns down projects that don't make sense.

WHAT THE SOLUTION LOOKS LIKE

Customer Communication

Order confirmations, status updates, follow-ups, review requests. The right message at the right time without someone remembering to send it.

Lead Routing & Follow-Up

New lead comes in, gets enriched with data, routed to the right rep, logged in your CRM, triggers a follow-up sequence. All automatic.

Reporting & Data Aggregation

Pull data from multiple sources, aggregate it, format it, deliver it. What takes 2-4 hours monthly can run automatically in 15 minutes.

Data Entry & Sync

When information lives in multiple places, keep it in sync automatically. No more copying between systems, no more version conflicts.

Onboarding Workflows

New customer or new employee - the checklist runs itself. Welcome emails, account setup, task creation, calendar invites. Nothing missed.

If fixing your process is smarter than automating it, I'll tell you. If the ROI doesn't make sense, I'll tell you. If you need something simpler than what I build, I'll point you to the right tool and save you the consulting fee.
