About

I'm Seth. I live in Texas with my family.

I spent a long time in a career that made sense on paper... good job, good paycheck, all the right boxes checked. But there was this quiet gap between what I was doing and what I actually wanted to be doing. Freelancing was how I started closing that gap.

Now I help businesses fix the technical stuff that wastes their time. Automation, CRM setups, tools that should talk to each other but don't. I've been at this long enough to know that most of the pain isn't a people problem... it's a systems problem. The people are usually fine. The process is what's broken.
