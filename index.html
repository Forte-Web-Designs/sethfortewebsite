Hundreds of markets analyzed daily, manual research eliminated

German Marketing Agency (Emergency Recovery)

83GB recovered, 43 workflows saved, 2 hours to full operation

The problem: Client's VPS hit 100% disk full. Docker crashed. Their entire n8n automation infrastructure was down, and they couldn't even SSH in to diagnose. Their business operations depended on those workflows running.

What I built: Diagnosed the issue (83GB SQLite database bloat from execution logs), found a creative solution using RAM as temporary backup space (the only space available), extracted all 43 workflows and 16 credentials, reset the database, restored everything, and configured auto-pruning to prevent recurrence.

The outcome: From "everything is down and we can't get in" to fully operational in 2 hours. 83GB recovered (disk went from 100% to 15%). All workflows and credentials preserved. Led to an ongoing development contract.

HOW THIS WORKS

I start by understanding what the problem is costing you. Hours per week, labor costs, missed opportunities. That's how we figure out if the investment makes sense and when you'll see ROI.

Step 1: Discovery Call (30 minutes, free)

We talk about what's slowing you down. I ask about your current workflow, where time disappears, and what you've tried. I'll tell you honestly whether automation makes sense for your situation.

Some problems need better process, not more automation. If I can't help you, I'll say so and point you in the right direction.

Step 2: Proposal (2-3 days)

If we're a good fit, I send a detailed proposal with clear deliverables, timeline, investment, and ROI calculation. You see exactly what you're getting and when it pays for itself.

No vague promises. Specific functionality, specific outcomes, specific milestones.

Step 3: Build (2-6 weeks depending on scope)

Build the system. Test with your team. Deploy when it's ready, not when a deadline says so.

This includes training your team, documenting everything, and making sure you can manage it without me if needed.

Step 4: Support (optional)

Available if you want it. Not required if you don't. Some clients prefer one-off projects. Others want ongoing optimization as their business evolves.

Results timeline

Most clients see measurable time savings within 30 days. Complete ROI typically happens in 4-8 weeks. I build things that pay for themselves fast, then keep delivering value.
