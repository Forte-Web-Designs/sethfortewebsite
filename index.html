    A little context

    I spent years doing work that looked right but didn't feel right. Good job, steady paycheck, checking boxes. The whole time there was this quiet voice asking... is this it?

    Freelancing was how I started answering that question. UpWork was the door. Somewhere along the way I got curious about how things actually work... not just the technical stuff, but all of it.

    Now I help businesses fix the technical problems that waste their time... automation, CRM setups, tools that actually talk to each other. But that's not what this site is about.

    This site is about the bigger thing. How to think about work. How to think about time. How to build something that matters while being present for the people you love.

    I live in Texas with my family. I'm still figuring it out. But I think the figuring out part is worth sharing.
