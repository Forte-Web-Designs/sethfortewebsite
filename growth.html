Add channels. Convert more. Handle the volume.

Leads come from one source. If that source goes down, your pipeline goes dry overnight. I add channels so you're pulling leads from multiple directions. Inbound captures people looking for you. Outbound goes and finds new ones. AI handles volume around the clock.

What I build

Meta Lead Ads to CRM

▼
You're running Meta ads and leads are sitting in a Facebook tab nobody checks. By the time you see them, they've already talked to your competitor. I connect your lead forms directly to your CRM with instant tagging, auto-response within 60 seconds, and follow-up that starts automatically.

Inbound Lead Capture

▼
A lead fills out your form at 2 PM. Nobody sees it until 5 PM. Your competitor responded in 3 minutes. I route every inbound source into one CRM with instant notifications, auto-response, missed lead alerts, and follow-up sequences. No lead sits untouched.

Outbound Cold Email

▼
Inbound is great until referrals dry up and ad costs climb. Outbound flips it: you go find them. I build the full pipeline with Apollo, AI personalization, verified sending, and reply routing to your CRM. 15-50 meetings per month at scale. Only makes sense at $3,000+ deal sizes.

AI Voice Agent

▼
Every missed call is a potential customer going to your competitor. I build an AI phone agent that answers 24/7, qualifies callers, answers FAQs, and books appointments on your calendar. Every call transcribed and logged in your CRM.

WhatsApp and Chat Intake

▼
Someone lands on your site at 9 PM with a question. No chat, no bot, no way to get an answer. They leave. I build a chat widget or WhatsApp integration with routing logic that captures leads and gets the right question to the right person, even when nobody's in the office.

Review and Reputation Automation

▼
You have 12 Google reviews. Your competitor has 87. Guess who gets the call. I automate review requests after service. Happy customers go to Google. Unhappy ones go to a private form so you handle it before it goes public.

Dispatch and Assignment

▼
A job comes in and someone spends 30 minutes searching a database, emailing people, and hoping for a response. I build a system that matches jobs to the best available worker, sends outreach in priority order, and confirms the assignment. I've built this for legal services, staffing, and mobile service dispatch.

AI Document Search

▼
Your most important information is buried in PDFs nobody can find and SOPs on one person's desktop. I build an AI search layer where your team asks questions in plain English and gets answers with the source document cited. Contract review that took an afternoon takes minutes.
