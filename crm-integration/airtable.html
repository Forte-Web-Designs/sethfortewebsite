Airtable Integration

Airtable is powerful for organizing data. But when it connects to your other tools and automates workflows? That's when it becomes the operational center of your business.

What I Build

CRM-Style Bases

Airtable as a lightweight CRM. Contacts, deals, pipelines - with automations that rival dedicated CRM software.

Project Trackers

Project management with custom views, automated status updates, and integrations to your other tools.

Inventory Systems

Track products, assets, equipment. QR codes, forms for check-in/out, automated alerts.

Content Calendars

Plan, track, and publish content. Integrate with social scheduling, team notifications, approval workflows.

Airtable Automations

  • Record created → Slack notification + email to assignee
  • Status changed → Update linked records + trigger next steps
  • Date approaching → Send reminders automatically
  • Form submission → Process data + assign tasks
  • Scheduled runs → Daily/weekly reports to stakeholders

Integrations

Connect Airtable to:

  • Communication: Slack, email, SMS
  • Forms: Typeform, JotForm, native Airtable forms
  • Docs: Google Docs, PDFs, document generation
  • Calendar: Google Calendar, scheduling tools
  • Payment: Stripe, invoicing systems
  • Custom: Any API via Make.com or n8n
