CRM Integration - One Source of Truth

Your CRM says one thing. Your spreadsheet says another. Someone's manually copying data between them. I fix that.

What I Integrate

CRMs

HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Close, Monday, Airtable - whatever you're using.

Email

Gmail, Outlook, ActiveCampaign, Mailchimp - synced with your customer data.

Forms

Typeform, JotForm, Gravity Forms, Webflow - leads flow directly to your CRM.

Accounting

QuickBooks, Xero, Stripe - sales data syncs to your books automatically.

Project Management

Asana, Monday, ClickUp, Notion - deals become projects without manual handoff.
