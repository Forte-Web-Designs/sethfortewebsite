When to Move Beyond Zapier

I'll tell you honestly if Zapier isn't the right fit:

  • High volume: When you're burning through tasks and the bill keeps climbing
  • Complex logic: When you need branching, loops, or error handling
  • Data transformation: When you need to manipulate data in complex ways
  • Multi-step dependencies: When steps depend on each other conditionally

In those cases, I'll recommend Make.com or n8n instead.
