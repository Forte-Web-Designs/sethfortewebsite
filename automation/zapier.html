Zapier Automation Expert

Zapier is the most popular automation tool for a reason: it works, it's reliable, and your team can understand it. Sometimes simple is the right answer.

When Zapier Is Right

Simple, Linear Workflows

Trigger → Action → Action. When your workflow is straightforward, Zapier handles it cleanly.

Team Maintenance

Your marketing or ops team will maintain these automations. Zapier's interface makes that possible.

Maximum Integration Coverage

5,000+ apps. If you're using niche tools, Zapier probably has a connector.

Need It Working Today

Fast to set up, reliable once running. When speed matters more than cost optimization.

Common Zapier Workflows

  • Form submission → CRM contact creation
  • New lead → Slack notification
  • Deal closed → Project creation in Asana/Monday
  • New subscriber → Welcome email sequence
  • Calendar event → Meeting notes template
  • Invoice paid → Update spreadsheet

When to Move Beyond Zapier

I'll tell you honestly if Zapier isn't the right fit:

  • High volume: When you're burning through tasks and the bill keeps climbing
  • Complex logic: When you need branching, loops, or error handling
  • Data transformation: When you need to manipulate data in complex ways
  • Multi-step dependencies: When steps depend on each other conditionally

In those cases, I'll recommend Make.com or n8n instead.
