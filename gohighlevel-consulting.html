GOHIGHLEVEL DOES A LOT. THAT'S THE PROBLEM.

CRM, funnels, email, SMS, automation, websites, appointment booking - it's an all-in-one platform. But setting it up to actually work for your business takes more than watching YouTube tutorials.

WHY GOHIGHLEVEL WORKS

GoHighLevel (GHL) is popular for good reasons:

  • All-in-one: CRM, marketing automation, funnels, websites, email, SMS - one platform instead of 10 subscriptions.
  • White-labeling: Agencies can rebrand it as their own platform for clients.
  • Workflows: Visual automation builder for lead nurturing, follow-ups, and operations.
  • Cost consolidation: Replace multiple tools with one monthly fee.

The challenge? GHL does so much that most people barely scratch the surface. They're paying for a Ferrari and driving it like a golf cart.

WHAT I BUILD

Workflow Automation

Lead nurturing sequences, follow-up automation, appointment reminders, re-engagement campaigns. Workflows that run reliably without manual intervention.

CRM Setup & Configuration

Pipeline setup, custom fields, tagging strategies, and lead scoring. A CRM that actually helps you close deals instead of just storing contacts.

API Integrations

Connecting GHL to your other systems. When native integrations aren't enough, I build custom API connections.

Migration

Moving from another CRM or marketing platform? I handle the migration - contacts, automations, and pipelines - with minimal disruption.

Agency Setup

White-label configuration, sub-account templates, and snapshot building for agencies that resell GHL to clients.

COMMON CHALLENGES I SOLVE

"I'm paying for GHL but barely using it." You signed up, built a few things, but never got the full automation system running. I get it set up properly.

"My workflows are a mess." You've built automations over time, and now it's spaghetti. I clean it up, consolidate, and make it maintainable.

"I need it to connect to other tools." GHL's native integrations don't cover everything. I build custom API connections to fill the gaps.

"I want to offer this to my clients." Agency setup - white-labeling, snapshots, sub-account structure. Build once, deploy to many.

"I'm moving from another platform." Migration from HubSpot, ActiveCampaign, or other CRMs without losing data or automation logic.

MY APPROACH

I build GHL systems that:

  • Actually get used - designed around how your team works, not how the tutorial said to do it
  • Run reliably - proper testing, error handling, and monitoring
  • Scale with you - architecture that doesn't break when you grow
