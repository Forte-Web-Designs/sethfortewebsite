Inventory Tracking Automation

Know exactly what you have, where it is, and when to reorder. No more manual counts. No more "I thought we had those."

The Problem

Spreadsheets say you have 50 units. The shelf has 12. Nobody knows where the rest went. Orders get oversold. Reorders happen too late.

Someone's doing weekly inventory counts that are outdated the moment they finish.

How It Works

Real-Time Tracking

Every sale, every receipt, every transfer updates inventory immediately. Always know what you have.

Multi-Location Support

Track inventory across warehouses, stores, vehicles - wherever your stuff lives.

Smart Alerts

Low stock warnings. Reorder triggers. Overstock notifications. Know before problems happen.

Mobile Access

Scan items in/out from phones. Check availability from anywhere. QR codes or barcodes.
