Email Automation

Customer abandons cart - reminder goes out. Lead downloads ebook - nurture sequence starts. Subscription renews - thank you email sends. All automatic.

The Problem

You're sending the same emails manually. Every welcome. Every follow-up. Every reminder. It's tedious, inconsistent, and things slip through the cracks.

Or you're blasting everyone with the same message at the same time, regardless of where they are in their journey.

How It Works

Behavioral Triggers

Emails sent based on what people do - sign up, purchase, visit a page, abandon a cart. Relevant and timely.

Smart Sequences

Multi-step campaigns that adapt. Skip emails if they've already converted. Branch based on engagement.

Personalization

Dynamic content based on CRM data. Not just "Hi {first_name}" - actual relevant messaging.

Cross-Platform

Works with your existing email tool. Klaviyo, ActiveCampaign, Mailchimp, HubSpot - we connect the data.

Real Example

Modern Bungalow

9 automated email scenarios: 15+ hours monthly saved

Order confirmations, shipping updates, back-in-stock notifications, review requests - all triggered automatically by Shopify events. Zero manual sending.

Common Email Automations

  • Welcome sequences: New subscriber/customer onboarding
  • Abandoned cart: Recovery emails with timing optimization
  • Post-purchase: Thank you, review requests, cross-sells
  • Re-engagement: Win back inactive customers
  • Transactional: Order confirmations, shipping updates
  • Lead nurture: Education sequences for prospects
