The Quality Question

"But won't it sound like AI?"

It can. Or it can sound exactly like you. The difference is in the build.

Generic AI content is obvious. Custom AI content - trained on your examples, your style, your brand voice - reads like something your team wrote.

I build the second kind.

When AI Content Makes Sense

High volume - More content than your team can reasonably create. Dozens or hundreds of pieces.

Structured format - Similar content types with variations. Product descriptions, not thought leadership.

Speed matters - Time-to-market is valuable. Listings need to go live now, not next week.

Consistency matters - Same quality across all outputs. No variance based on who wrote it or what day it was.

Investment

AI content systems typically range from $4,000 to $15,000 depending on complexity and volume requirements.

The ROI is usually straightforward: compare cost of the system to cost of doing it manually. Most pay for themselves in 1-3 months.
