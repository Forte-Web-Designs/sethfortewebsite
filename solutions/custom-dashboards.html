Custom Dashboards & Internal Tools

You need to see what's happening in your business. But the reports from your existing tools don't show what actually matters. Or they require 30 minutes of exporting and formatting to be useful.

The Problem with Off-the-Shelf

Every SaaS tool has dashboards. They're fine for generic use cases. But your business isn't generic.

You need to see data from multiple systems in one place. You need calculations that your tools don't support. You need views that match how you actually make decisions - not how the software vendor thinks you should.

You end up exporting to spreadsheets, building manual reports, or just not having visibility into things that matter.

What I Build

Executive Dashboards

The 5 numbers that actually matter, updated automatically. No more asking "can someone pull the latest data?"

Operational Dashboards

Real-time visibility into what's happening now. Inventory levels, order status, team capacity, pipeline health.

Client-Facing Portals

Give customers visibility into their projects, orders, or accounts. Professional, branded, always current.

Internal Tools

Custom apps for your specific workflows. Quote calculators, scheduling tools, inventory management, approval systems.

How This Works

Understand - What decisions are you trying to make? What information do you need? What's currently frustrating about getting it?

Design - Sketch out what you actually need to see. Not what looks impressive - what's useful.

Connect - Pull data from your existing systems. CRM, accounting, project management, spreadsheets - wherever it lives.

Build - Create the dashboard or tool. Clean, fast, focused on what matters.

Iterate - Use it for a week, then refine. The first version is never perfect. That's fine.

Examples

Agency owner - Needed to see profitability by client, factoring in time tracked across tools, contractor costs, and retainer values. Built a dashboard that calculates real margins automatically.

E-commerce operator - Needed inventory visibility across warehouse, Amazon, and Shopify with reorder alerts. Built a unified view with automatic low-stock notifications.

Service business - Needed a client portal showing project status, upcoming milestones, and shared documents. Built a branded portal that updates automatically from their project management tool.

Investment

Custom dashboard projects typically range from $4,000 to $15,000 depending on complexity and number of data sources.

The ROI is usually less about cost savings and more about clarity. Better decisions. Faster responses. Problems caught before they become crises.
