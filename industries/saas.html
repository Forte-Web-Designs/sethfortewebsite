SaaS Automation

Users sign up but don't activate. Churn happens silently. Your CS team is reactive instead of proactive. Product data sits in silos. Let's connect everything.

SaaS Problems I Solve

User Onboarding

Behavioral triggers based on product usage. User completes step 1? Prompt step 2. User stalls? Reach out with help.

Churn Signals

Identify at-risk customers before they leave. Usage drops, logins decrease, feature adoption stalls - get alerted.

Usage-Based Triggers

Approaching plan limits? Upsell prompt. Hit a milestone? Celebrate and cross-sell. All automatic.

Product-Sales Sync

Product usage data in your CRM. Sales knows who's engaged before they call. CS sees the full picture.

Billing Automation

Stripe events trigger workflows. Upgrade, downgrade, failed payment - appropriate responses automated.
