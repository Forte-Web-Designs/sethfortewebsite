YOU CHOSE N8N. GOOD CALL.

You wanted more control than Zapier offers. Maybe you needed self-hosting. Maybe you hit the limits of simpler tools. Now you need someone who can actually build it right.

WHY YOU CHOSE N8N

n8n isn't the easy choice. It's the powerful one. You chose it because:

  • Self-hosting: Your data stays on your servers. No third-party access.
  • No per-execution fees: Run unlimited workflows without watching costs scale.
  • Complex logic: When visual builders feel limiting, n8n's code nodes let you do anything.
  • Developer-friendly: Version control, custom nodes, API access.

These are the right reasons. But n8n's power comes with complexity. That's where I come in.

WHAT I SOLVE

Hosting Decisions

Complex Workflows

Error Handling

Performance & Scale

Workflows that run fast and don't choke under load. Queue management, parallel processing, database optimization.

Emergency Recovery
