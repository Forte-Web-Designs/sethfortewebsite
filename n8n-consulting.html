N8N IN ACTION

KlnBros Agency - Emergency Recovery

From "everything is down" to fully operational in 2 hours

The problem: Server crashed. 100% disk full. Entire n8n automation infrastructure down. Couldn't even SSH in.

What they got: Diagnosed the issue (83GB SQLite database bloat), found creative solution using RAM as temporary space, extracted all 43 workflows and 16 credentials, restored everything.

The outcome: Fully operational in 2 hours. 83GB recovered. All workflows preserved. Configured auto-pruning to prevent recurrence.

"Communication was 10/10. I felt very safe throughout the entire project knowing Seth was handling it."

Prediction Market Trader - AI Forecasting Pipeline

Hundreds of markets analyzed daily - research that runs while you sleep

The problem: Manually researching hundreds of prediction markets. Reading reports, checking data, forecasting one at a time.

What they got: n8n pipeline that pulls markets from Kalshi's API, enriches with company data, runs AI forecasting, outputs to spreadsheet with confidence scores.

The outcome: Hundreds of markets analyzed daily. Focus on decisions, not data gathering.

"Figured it out quickly and got everything working smoothly. Very responsive throughout."
