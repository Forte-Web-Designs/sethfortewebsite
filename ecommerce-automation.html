YOU'RE SELLING. NOW YOU'RE DROWNING.

Orders are coming in. That's the good news. The bad news? Customer emails, inventory updates, product listings, returns - the work that comes after scales linearly with revenue while your margins stay flat.

THE E-COMMERCE OPERATIONS PROBLEM

Every order generates work that has nothing to do with selling:

  • Customer communication: Order confirmations, shipping updates, delivery notifications, review requests. Someone has to send them - or they don't get sent.
  • Inventory management: Updating quantities across channels, reordering when stock gets low, syncing between warehouse and store.
  • Product listings: Photos need processing, descriptions need writing, listings need posting - across Shopify, Amazon, eBay, wherever you sell.
  • Returns and refunds: Processing requests, updating inventory, handling customer communication.

This work doesn't generate revenue. It's the cost of generating revenue. The more you sell, the more it costs - unless you automate.
