THE AGENCY OPERATIONS PROBLEM

Every new client adds more than just billable work:

  • Reporting: Pulling data from multiple platforms, organizing it, calculating metrics, making it look presentable. Every. Single. Month.
  • Client onboarding: Access requests, asset collection, account setup, welcome sequences. The same checklist, over and over.
  • Project management: Creating tasks, assigning work, tracking progress, updating clients. Administrative overhead on top of actual work.
  • Invoicing and billing: Tracking hours, calculating fees, generating invoices, chasing payments.

This is the work that doesn't scale. Add 10 clients, add 10x the reporting. Unless you automate.

WHAT RESULTS LOOK LIKE

Kingfisher Ads

Monthly reporting that takes 15 minutes instead of half a day

The problem: Manually pulling ad spend from Meta and Google Ads monthly for each client. Organizing spreadsheets, calculating management fees, preparing invoice-ready reports. 2-4 hours every month.
